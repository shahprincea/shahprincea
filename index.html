How to Write Effective Prompts

Writing prompts effectively is key to getting accurate and actionable responses from AI systems like ChatGPT or Anthropic. Here are some tips:

  • Be specific and concise about your requirements.
  • Provide examples or structured templates for clarity.
  • Ask for outputs in a desired format, such as JSON, lists, or paragraphs.
  • Iterate on your prompt based on the responses you get.
